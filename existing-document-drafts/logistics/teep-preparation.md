# TEEP Preparation Overview
TEEP stands for 'Test Environment Establishment Procedure'. It is a crucial process in the test and validation of RAN (Radio Access Network) equipment.

The goal of TEEP is to establish a stable and repeatable test environment that allows for accurate testing of RAN equipment.

This section will provide an overview of the TEEP process, including its objectives, steps, and best practices.
